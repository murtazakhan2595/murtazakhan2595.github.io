Breakfast

Recipe Ingredients Instructions
Avocado Toast
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
 Mash avocado, squeeze lemon, add salt and pepper, spread on toasted bread. Enjoy!
Blueberry Pancakes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
 Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, fold in blueberries, cook on a greased pan until golden brown. Serve hot!

Lunch

Recipe Image Ingredients Instructions
Greek Salad
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
 Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Serve chilled!
Grilled Cheese Sandwich
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
 Butter one side of each bread slice. Place cheese between slices, buttered side out. Cook in a pan until golden brown on both sides. Enjoy!

Dinner

Recipe Image Ingredients Instructions
Spaghetti Carbonara
  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 slices of bacon, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
 Cook spaghetti. In a pan, cook bacon until crispy. In a bowl, whisk eggs and cheese. Toss hot spaghetti with egg mixture and bacon. Season and serve!
Vegetable Stir Fry
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • Cooking oil
 Heat oil in a pan. Add garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant. Add vegetables, stir-fry until tender. Add soy sauce and sesame oil. Serve hot!

Dessert

Recipe Image Ingredients Instructions
Chocolate Chip Cookies
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
 Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix dry ingredients separately, then combine. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake at 350°F for 10-12 mins. Enjoy with milk!
Ice Cream Sundae
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Cherries
  • Sprinkles
 Scoop ice cream into a bowl. Drizzle with chocolate syrup. Top with whipped cream, cherries, and sprinkles. Indulge immediately!
